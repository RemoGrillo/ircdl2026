Archival Knowledge Graphs

Balancing Semantic Richness and Accessibility
of Hierarchical Structures

Remo Grillo · Lucia Giagnolini · Paolo Bonora
Alma Mater Studiorum — Università di Bologna · /DH.ARC
IRCDL 2026 · Modena · February 19–20, 2026
Good morning everyone. My name is Remo Grillo, and I'm presenting joint work with Lucia Giagnolini and Paolo Bonora from the University of Bologna. Our paper addresses a fundamental challenge: how do we make large-scale archival knowledge graphs navigable for users who are not experts in semantic technologies? We propose a solution called the Semantic Tree Advanced, and we tested it on the born-digital archive of Italian writer Valerio Evangelisti.
