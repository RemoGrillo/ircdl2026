The platform we use is ResearchSpace, an open-source environment for managing and exploring cultural heritage knowledge graphs. It was developed at the British Museum with support from the Mellon Foundation and is now maintained by Kartography CIC. ResearchSpace is built on RDF and SPARQL and offers a modular, template-based architecture. Importantly, it was designed and tested primarily with CIDOC CRM—the museum-oriented standard. Our project represents one of the first implementations using an archival ontology, specifically BoDi extending RiC-O, which allowed us to test the platform's flexibility beyond its original museum-oriented scope. This is a significant contribution: demonstrating that ResearchSpace can support archival ontological frameworks.
